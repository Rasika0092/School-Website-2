Mr.Abinath

Mr.Abinath

If someone asks me what’s the formula for success, I would put it as the product of passion and perseverance nurtured in a positive ecosystem. I ardently believe that success belongs to those who believe in themselves. To try out this simple equation of mine, I had been showered with the blessings of Almighty and my Godly mother with this opportunity to spear head our Queen Mira International School (QMIS) in the path of accomplishment since 2009. At QMIS we deeply instill the belief in Indian values yet with an international outlook. Confidence is instilled upon individuals by providing them a learning ambience where they break the barrier of fear and explore their inquisitiveness. Providing an ecosystem for a happy schooling where learning is taken “more & beyond” books and classrooms was the only objective of mine, when the concept of Queen Mira was envisioned in my mind. Today we are in the path of realizing our vision of “Educating the leaders of tomorrow who will change the way the world lives, learns and works”, through our blended curriculum , competent resource team and a well sculpted student enrichment programs. Team QMIS is dedicated to enable our children to develop to identity for themselves, realize their true potential, identity and fulfil their aspirations and take an active and constructive role in the global development. I invite you all to be a part of this life long journey of learning. In the journey of making the change makers. “எண்ணிய எண்ணியாங்கு எய்துவர் எண்ணியார் திண்ணியர் ஆகப் பெறின்” ( குறள்: # 666).

Dr.Chandran

Dr.Chandran

I still remember the days in the year 1996 when I questioned myself, “What is your contribution to the society?” After deliberating for quite some time, I felt that the only way to uplift the society is to enlighten the students community through quality education and hence started a learning process of finding what the education in the country was, is and how it should be. Education is not just about imparting knowledge but its training to younger minds to develop and cultivate skills of utilizing the processed knowledge meaningfully and effectively towards the betterment of self and society. The global outlook, a spirit of equality, concern for man and environment and a quest for excellence are the hallmarks, which have come alive at our QMIS..

Ms.Sujatha

Ms.Sujatha

It is our core aspiration that our students cultivate and explore their innate talents and interests. Children of today need a system of learning that is in line with the information age and anywhere anytime learning opportunity. Children at QMIS are at an advantage of learning through this experience. The inspiring learning ambience, personalized attention by passionate people, various student enrichment programs and the 3 B curriculum of QMIS amuse the learners as these features take their learning more and beyond the classroom. Joy is instilled in everything they do and learning is fun at QMIS as we believe the child’s happiness is as important as their acquisition of knowledge and understanding. While we have high expectations and ambitions for our students, we also know that for them to succeed we need to provide a caring, supportive and challenging environment in which they can grow and flourish. Wishing all the children a happy schooling at this flourishing ambience. “உள்ளுவ தெல்லாம் உயர்வுள்ளல் மற்றது தள்ளினுந் தள்ளாமை நீர்த்து”( குறள்: # 596)
